# Privacy Policy — RecoveryIQ

**Last updated: March 2, 2026**

## Summary

RecoveryIQ does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
Everything stays on your device.

## Data Collection

RecoveryIQ collects no data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in,
and no backend servers. Nothing leaves your iPhone.

## Apple Health Data

RecoveryIQ reads heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate, sleep,
and workout data from Apple Health to calculate your daily readiness score.
This data:

- Is read locally on your device only
- Is never transmitted off your device
- Is never shared with any third party
- Is stored in a local database on your iPhone only

## Local Storage

All app data — readiness scores, workout logs, and settings — is stored
in a local database on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

## Third-Party Services

RecoveryIQ contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
No data is sent to any external service.
